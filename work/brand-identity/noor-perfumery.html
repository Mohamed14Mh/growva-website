Luxury Fragrance

Noor Perfumery Identity.

A full brand system — wordmark, monogram, typographic hierarchy, and a refined palette — built for an artisan fragrance house expanding into retail and hospitality.

Challenge

Noor had a name and a recipe, but no identity. Their existing logo was clip art in Arial. They needed to enter luxury retail and gift-buying channels within six months and had no visual foundation to do either.

Strategy

We built the brand system in three phases: identity audit and positioning, logo and wordmark development (12 directions narrowed to 3 refined options), and system rollout — type scale, colour palette, photographic direction, and packaging specifications. The final identity referenced calligraphic Arabic letterforms without mimicking them.

Result

The brand launched into two luxury retail locations and a hospitality brand partnership within four months. Wholesale enquiries doubled in the first quarter after the rebrand. The identity is now in use across Shopify, Amazon, Instagram, and physical packaging.
