Luxury Homewares

Atelier Marbre Packaging.

Tactile product packaging for marble objets — matte laminate, silk-screen print, custom tissue insert — designed for retail, gifting, and DHL Worlwide.

Challenge

Atelier Marbre's pieces arrived in bubble wrap inside generic white boxes. At a retail price of AED 850–4,200, buyers expected packaging that matched the product's craft. Several wholesale accounts cited packaging as a barrier to listing.

Strategy

We designed a rigid box with a matte laminate exterior and a silk-screen printed wordmark in a single white pass. The interior was a two-tone tissue system with a printed tissue and a branded sticker seal. A custom foam insert was designed for each piece size. The full system was engineered to DHL shipping standards.

Result

Three wholesale accounts that had declined listed within two months of the packaging launch. Returns citing transit damage fell from 4.2% to 0.3%. The packaging has been featured in three product photography contexts.
