Luxury Brand

Atelier Marbre Website.

A bespoke brand website with editorial photography direction, collection pages, and a custom bespoke enquiry system — built to match the product's price point.

Challenge

Atelier Marbre had no website. Enquiries came through Instagram DM and word of mouth. As they entered a wholesale and hospitality channel, buyers required a web presence that communicated the brand's craft and production process.

Strategy

We structured the site around four sections: brand story, material world (a visual explainer of marble finishes and origin), collection (with individual product pages and a bespoke enquiry form), and press. Photography direction and prop styling guidance was produced for the client's own shoot. The site was built on a custom CMS.

Result

Time-on-site averaged 4m 20s. Wholesale enquiries via the site generated three confirmed accounts in the first two months. The site was submitted to and featured in one design publication within six weeks of launch.
