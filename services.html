Premium Shopify Website Development

Most Shopify stores look like Shopify stores. Ours don't. We build custom storefronts from scratch — no bloated themes, no template compromises — designed specifically for your product, your customer, and your conversion goals.

The Problem

Generic templates constrain your brand identity, load slowly, and force your customer journey to fit a mold that was designed for someone else's product.

Our Solution

A fully custom Shopify build — design, development, and UX — planned around how your specific customer makes a purchase decision in your category.

Deliverables

  • Custom Liquid theme
  • Responsive across all devices
  • Conversion-optimized page templates
  • Shopify integrations configured
  • Performance-tested at launch

Ideal Client

Product brands in perfume, cosmetics, fashion, food & beverage, or agriculture who want a store that looks as premium as the product inside it.

Expected Outcome

A storefront that converts at a higher rate, loads faster, and positions the brand at the level the product deserves — from day one.

Our Process

  • Discovery and brand audit
  • UX strategy and wireframing
  • High-fidelity design in Figma
  • Custom Liquid development
  • QA, performance testing, launch
Timeline: 6–10 weeks from kickoff to launch

Shopify CRO

Traffic is expensive. Losing it to a broken checkout or a confusing product page is more expensive. We analyze where your funnel breaks, then rebuild those points systematically.

The Problem

Visitors arrive but don't convert. The issue is rarely the product — it's the friction points between landing and checkout that the existing store never fixed.

Our Solution

Funnel analysis, heatmap review, session recordings, and A/B testing translated into specific design and copy changes with measurable lift.

Deliverables

  • Full funnel audit report
  • Priority list of friction points
  • Redesigned product and cart pages
  • A/B test setup and results
  • Monthly optimization roadmap

Ideal Client

Established stores with consistent traffic (500+ sessions/day) that are under-converting relative to industry benchmarks.

Expected Outcome

Measurable increase in conversion rate, add-to-cart rate, and checkout completion within the first 60–90 days of optimization.

Our Process

  • Analytics and session review
  • Hypothesis development
  • Design and implementation
  • A/B or multivariate testing
  • Results analysis and next cycle
Timeline: Ongoing monthly retainer or 6-week sprint

Shopify Store Optimization

An existing store with the wrong structure, too many apps, and neglected technical debt performs worse every month. We audit, clean, and restructure it so it runs the way it should.

The Problem

Stores built quickly or by multiple different developers accumulate technical debt — slow load times, conflicting apps, broken mobile layouts, and SEO issues that compound over time.

Our Solution

A structured technical audit followed by a clean-up and rebuild of the areas causing the most drag — speed, structure, or UX depending on what the data shows.

Deliverables

  • Full technical audit report
  • App audit and trimming plan
  • Speed optimization implementation
  • Mobile UX corrections
  • SEO technical fixes

Ideal Client

Stores 12+ months old that have never had a technical audit, or stores built on a template that's now causing more problems than it solves.

Expected Outcome

Faster page loads, lower bounce rate, and a store that doesn't actively cost sales through technical friction.

Our Process

  • Core Web Vitals baseline
  • App and code audit
  • Prioritized fix list
  • Implementation and QA
  • Post-fix performance report
Timeline: 3–5 weeks for audit + implementation

Performance Optimization

Core Web Vitals, image pipelines, and app-bloat audits that keep the store fast as it grows. A 1-second delay in page load time reduces conversions by up to 7%.

The Problem

Every new app, unoptimized image, and third-party script added to a store adds load time. Over months, a fast store becomes a slow one without anyone noticing until the conversion rate drops.

Our Solution

A comprehensive performance audit and implementation sprint targeting Core Web Vitals, image compression, script loading order, and app consolidation.

Deliverables

  • Core Web Vitals baseline report
  • Image optimization (entire catalog)
  • Script audit and lazy-loading
  • App consolidation recommendations
  • Post-implementation benchmark

Ideal Client

Any Shopify store scoring below 70 on PageSpeed Insights or seeing checkout abandonment that correlates with slow page transitions.

Expected Outcome

PageSpeed score improvement of 20–40 points, measurable reduction in Time to First Byte and Largest Contentful Paint, and conversion rate lift.

Our Process

  • Lighthouse and CWV audit
  • Bottleneck identification
  • Implementation sprint
  • CDN and caching review
  • Final performance report
Timeline: 2–4 weeks
