Pricing & Process

Every project is scoped individually after a discovery conversation. We give you a fixed written proposal before any work begins — no open-ended hourly billing, no "scope creep" surprises mid-project. The number we agree on is the number you pay, unless the scope changes by mutual agreement.

Yes — typically 50% at project kickoff and 50% at launch. For larger projects, we sometimes use a three-milestone structure (kickoff, design approval, launch). Payment terms are always agreed before work begins and written into the project contract.

It starts with a conversation — usually 45–60 minutes — where we learn about the brand, the current situation, and what success looks like. No pitch, no template proposal. After that, we'll share a written brief, agree on scope, and present a fixed proposal. Discovery is billed separately for larger strategic engagements; for development projects, it's typically included.

Two rounds of design revisions are included in all packages. Additional revision rounds are available at a defined rate if needed.
