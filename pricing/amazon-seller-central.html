Performance-Based Model

Performance Partnership

Starting from 12,000EGP + 2.5% of Net Amazon Sales

Available to select brands after reviewing the account and products.

Includes: full account management, product and content management, advertising campaign management, a monthly growth plan, and ongoing reporting. Minimum contract term: 6 months.

Net Amazon Sales refers to the value of sales after cancelled orders and returns, before Amazon's commissions and fees are deducted.

This model isn't offered to every client — it's used only once we've confirmed the product is strong, inventory is available, and pricing is appropriate.

Advertising Management

Advertising Management Beyond Package Limits

Advertising budget itself is not included and is paid directly by the client to Amazon.

If your ad spend exceeds the limit included in your package: 8% of the excess ad spend.

Example: the Growth package includes management up to 75,000 EGP. If the client spends 100,000 EGP, the 8% is calculated only on the extra 25,000 EGP.

Contract Terms

How billing and contract terms work

  • Minimum monthly contract term: 3 months.
  • Performance and Enterprise contracts: minimum 6 months.
  • The monthly subscription is paid in advance.
  • 5% discount when prepaying 6 months.
  • 10% discount when prepaying 12 months.
  • One-time launch packages: 50% upfront, 50% before final publish.
  • Delivery timelines start after we receive products, information, and documents.
  • Unused products within a subscription do not roll over to the next month.
  • Design work includes 2 rounds of revisions.
  • Reshoots due to product or packaging changes are billed separately.

Scope

What's Not Included

  • Amazon fees and commissions.
  • Advertising budget.
  • FBA, storage, and shipping costs.
  • Cost of transporting products to and from the studio.
  • Government taxes.
  • Barcodes or trademark registration.
  • Models, locations, or special props.
  • Physical packaging and printing.
  • Order packing/fulfillment service inside the client's own warehouse.
  • Any services in additional marketplaces not listed in the package.
