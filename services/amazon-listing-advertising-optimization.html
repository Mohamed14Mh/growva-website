Amazon Listing & Advertising Optimization

Amazon visibility is earned through precise listing content, keyword structure, image hierarchy, and PPC management. We optimize the product page and the paid traffic system together.

The Problem

Strong products lose to weaker competitors when titles are unfocused, images do not answer objections, keywords are thin, and PPC campaigns spend without learning.

Our Solution

A listing and advertising sprint that improves search relevance, conversion content, campaign structure, and reporting so paid spend becomes a learning system.

Deliverables

  • Keyword research and listing map
  • Title, bullets, and description optimization
  • Image and A+ content recommendations
  • PPC campaign structure
  • Search term and performance report

Ideal Client

Amazon sellers with live listings and traffic but weak conversion, inefficient ad spend, or unclear keyword positioning.

Expected Outcome

Better listing clarity, stronger keyword coverage, cleaner PPC structure, and a measurable path toward improved ACOS and conversion rate.

Our Process

  • Listing and ad account audit
  • Keyword and competitor research
  • Content rewrite and creative direction
  • PPC restructure and launch
  • Optimization review cycle
Timeline: 3–6 weeks + ongoing optimization
