Custom Store Development

Shopify is often the right commerce choice, but not always. For brands with unusual operational, content, marketplace, or platform requirements, we build custom store experiences outside Shopify when the brief calls for it.

The Problem

Some commerce requirements do not fit neatly inside a standard platform: complex catalogs, gated buying flows, unusual fulfillment rules, or product configuration logic that needs more control.

Our Solution

A custom commerce build scoped around the operational reality of the business, with careful platform selection, clean architecture, and a clear maintenance plan.

Deliverables

  • Platform and architecture recommendation
  • Custom frontend and backend scope
  • Product and checkout logic
  • Third-party integrations
  • QA and launch documentation

Ideal Client

Brands with commerce requirements that cannot be solved well through Shopify, Amazon, or a conventional plugin stack.

Expected Outcome

A commerce system built around the way the business actually sells, without forcing the customer journey through the wrong platform assumptions.

Our Process

  • Requirements and platform discovery
  • Technical architecture
  • UX and design system
  • Development and integration
  • Testing, launch, and handover
Timeline: 8–14 weeks depending on scope
